About Us

Welcome to heart me out – a place where you can find support, understanding, and a safe space to share your thoughts. I’m Manish Singh, a tech enthusiast and an explorer at heart. Having completed my BCA from Kumaun University and currently pursuing an MCA from Galgotia University, I have a strong passion for technology and innovation. But beyond tech, I’ve always believed in the power of connection and the importance of having someone to talk to during challenging times. This platform is my way of giving back to society by creating a space where anyone in need of counseling or just a listening ear can find help. Whether you're dealing with stress, navigating life's uncertainties, or simply need someone to hear you out, we are here to support you. At heart me out, we aim to provide: Confidentiality: A judgment-free zone to share your thoughts. Empathy: A compassionate approach to understanding your needs. Support: Resources and guidance to help you navigate your challenges. Life is a journey, and sometimes we all need a little help along the way. This website is built with the vision of fostering meaningful conversations and helping people find their strength. Let’s work together to make this a world where no one feels unheard or alone. Feel free to reach out and start a conversation – because every voice deserves to be heard.
